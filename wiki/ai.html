Bosses and Enemies

All maps have a unique boss that spawns, along with its own guards. Additionally, some AI are only found in certain maps, making them map-exclusive.

Since this game is strictly PVE, you will find most enemies having the ability to damage you from afar.

The game's AI has several different kinds of behaviour, and is listed below:

  • Sniper, these AI will cautiously approach you before firing a shot, then flee.
  • Grunt, these AI will slowly move toward you while providing supressive fire.
  • Shotgunner, these AI will quickly rush you and in most cases 2 tap you.
  • Random, completely random movement.
  • Others; these AI might do things like throw grenades or have hybrid behaviour types.
